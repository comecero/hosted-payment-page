Using Hosted Payment Page

Hosted Payment Page is an easy way to collect direct payments from your customers without a cart or invoice. Using Hosted Payment Page is easy!

Click the button to see Hosted Payment Page in action. Keep reading for more details on how to customize it to meet your needs.

Creating Links to Hosted Payment Page

You can create links manually using the documentation provided below, or even easier, use the Link Builder tool.

Customization Options

Hosted Payment Page allows a number of customizations, such as:

  • Full control over the design, colors, logo, etc.
  • Collect payment in multiple currencies
  • Multi-language order form
  • Payments by credit card or PayPal with others to be added in the future
  • Allow customers to save their payment method with a username and password for speedy future payments
  • Collect custom data from customers with custom fields that can be optional or required
  • Support for a custom payment reference (identifier) and / or description, either provided by you or requested from the customer
  • Support to pre-populate the payment amount, or have the customer provide the amount directly
  • Support to pre-populate shipping and tax amounts, for reporting and tracking
  • Provide additional custom data with the payment for your reference

To set your preferences, login to your account, go to Apps> Hosted Payment Page. Click on the arrow next to "Launch" and select "Settings" and "Style"

Creating Links Manually

Below are several examples, you can mix amd match parameters to provide the options you want.

A basic payment page where the customer supplies the amount and currency
{{data.src}}/#/link
This URL has no parameters and the user will be asked to supply the amount to pay directly.

Supply the amount, allow the user to change the amount
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&total=100.00
If you don't supply the currency, your account's default payment currency will be used.

Supply the amount, don't allow the user to change the amount
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&subtotal=100.00
By providing the amount as a subtotal, the amount on the page will not be editable by the user.

Supply the amount, shipping and tax
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&subtotal=100.00&shipping=5&tax=7.97
If you provide shipping and / or tax, subtotal is required. The total will be calculated and shown to the user.

Supply the amount, payment reference and description
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&total=100.00&reference=ABCD-1234&description=July+Invoice+Payment
The reference and description should be URL encoded.

Supply the amount, and customer data
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&total=100.00&name=John+Doe&company_name=Acme+Inc
You can provide name, company_name and email

Any other parameters you provide will be saved with the payment as meta data
{{data.src}}/#/link?currency=USD&total=100.00&my-data=whatever&this=that&more=stuff
The values you provide will be recorded and available for your records when you view the payment.
